My hometown - Vietnam

Allow me to introduce you to the heart and soul of my world – Vietnam. This enchanting country, nestled in Southeast Asia, is the place I call home. From the bustling streets of Hanoi or Ho Chi Minh City, to the peaceful waters of Ha Long Bay, Da Nang, Nha Trang,... Vietnam is a tapestry of history, culture and breathtaking scenery. If you get the chance to come here, I guarantee you will discover hidden gems, enjoy delicious food and bask in the warm embrace of Vietnamese hospitality. First embark on a virtual tour of my beloved hometown, where every street corner is a testament to the beauty of contrasts and the harmony of tradition.

Hanoi

Welcome to Hanoi, the charming capital of Vietnam. With bustling streets, ancient pagodas and lively markets, Hanoi is a city where the past gracefully intertwines with the present. As you stroll along the old town, the aroma of street food fills the air and echoes of history echo through the charming alleys. Explore this dynamic city with me, where every nook and cranny has a story waiting to be discovered. I had the opportunity to visit Ha Noi in the fall of 2020. The cool and pleasant weather made walking around the old quarter very comfortable. When you come here, don't hesitate to try Vietnam's specialty Pho, you won't be able to resist its deliciousness, and many other great dishes also.

Ho Chi Minh City

Ho Chi Minh City, formerly known as Saigon, is the largest and most populous city in Vietnam. Located in the southern part of the country, it serves as a bustling economic and cultural hub. With its energetic atmosphere, modern skyline, and historical significance, Ho Chi Minh City offers a captivating blend of old-world charm and contemporary vibrancy.

Dalat

Nestled in the Central Highlands of Vietnam, Dalat is a picturesque escape that feels like a fairytale come to life. With its temperate climate, rolling hills, and French colonial architecture, this city is a refreshing departure from the ordinary. Known as the "City of Eternal Spring," Đà Lạt invites you to explore its lush gardens, serene lakes, and romantic charm. Join me as we discover the tranquility and beauty of this highland haven, where nature's artistry and human creativity converge in perfect harmony.

Vietnam is a worthy place for you to come and experience. There are still many places on this S-shaped strip of land that I have not yet been to, but that is definitely something I will do in the near future. How about you, drop Vietnam off your bucket list!
