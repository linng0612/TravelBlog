Tallinn in Estonia in a winter day

The first trip of 2023 is to Estonia. This is a short trip of 1 day and 1 night, I think this is enough time to experience Tallinn. On a day in mid-January, when the Christmas market was still there but it was no longer crowded and bustling. I walked through the old streets, stopped by cute souvenir shops, and especially ate pancakes at Pub Kompressor because it is so good.
